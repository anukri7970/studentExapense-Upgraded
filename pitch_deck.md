# StudentXpense AI - Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Title Slide
**StudentXpense AI**
*Transparent, Smart, and Secure Student Allowance Management*
Subtitle: "Bridging the trust gap between parents and students with Stellar Smart Contracts and AI."

## Slide 2: The Problem
* **No Visibility:** Once parents send money for rent or tuition, they lose track of how it’s actually spent.
* **Lack of Financial Guidance:** Students struggle to budget effectively and often rely on generic advice that doesn't fit their real spending habits.
* **Friction in Payments:** Traditional banking apps move money but don't provide categorized tracking or purpose-driven escrows.

## Slide 3: The Solution
* **Smart Escrow Contracts:** Parents lock funds on the Stellar network designated for specific students.
* **On-Chain Transparency:** Every transaction is recorded on the public ledger, allowing verifiable proof of where funds go.
* **AI Budget Advisor:** An integrated AI analyzes real spending patterns and provides personalized, actionable advice for the student.

## Slide 4: Market Opportunity
* **Target Audience:** Millions of university students and parents globally who transfer allowances regularly.
* **Growing Web3 Adoption:** Increasing comfort with blockchain-based financial tools.
* **Gap in the Market:** Existing apps either do *only* budgeting (Mint, YNAB) or *only* transfers (Venmo, Zelle). StudentXpense merges both.

## Slide 5: Product Architecture
* **Frontend:** Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS (Sleek Dark Mode UI)
* **Backend:** Express, MongoDB (for fast querying and off-chain data)
* **Smart Contracts:** Soroban / Rust (Escrow management, secure releases)
* **AI Engine:** Gemini AI for tailored budget recommendations
